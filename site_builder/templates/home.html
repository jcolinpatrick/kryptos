What is Kryptos K4?

Kryptos is an encrypted sculpture at CIA headquarters containing four messages. K1–K3 were solved in 1999. K4 — 97 characters — has resisted all public attempts for 35 years. We know 24 characters: EASTNORTHEAST at positions 21–33 and BERLINCLOCK at positions 63–73.
