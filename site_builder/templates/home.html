About This Project

Jim Sanborn designed K4 to be solved. This site documents every approach that has been tried and eliminated, so the community can focus on what remains untested. Built by Colin Patrick and Claude (Anthropic). Read more →

What is Kryptos K4?

Kryptos is an encrypted sculpture at CIA headquarters containing four messages. K1–K3 were solved in 1999. K4 — 97 characters — has resisted all public cryptanalytic attempts for 35 years. 24 plaintext characters are known: EASTNORTHEAST at positions 21–33 and BERLINCLOCK at positions 63–73.
